DB2 11.5 – FULL TABLESPACE RESTORE LIVE DEMO
Environment: KSLAB1
Scenario: Restore TS2 as a whole tablespace

--------------------------------------------------
1. INTRODUCTION
--------------------------------------------------

Explain:
"In this scenario, I am not recovering a single row.
I am restoring the whole TS2 tablespace and bringing it
forward to the latest consistent state."

--------------------------------------------------
2. PRE-CHECK
--------------------------------------------------

db2 connect to KSLAB1

db2 "select * from T_TS2 order by ID"

db2 list history backup all for KSLAB1

Explain:
"We confirm the objects in TS2 and verify the backup image
that will be used for the restore."

Important backup:
ONLINE FULL = 20260217102911

--------------------------------------------------
3. TAKE TS2 OFFLINE
--------------------------------------------------

db2 "set tablespace TS2 offline"

Explain:
"We isolate the affected tablespace before recovery."

--------------------------------------------------
4. RESTORE THE WHOLE TABLESPACE
--------------------------------------------------

db2 restore db KSLAB1 \
tablespace (TS2) \
online \
from /ars/home/kslab/backup/full/tgt1,/ars/home/kslab/backup/full/tgt2 \
taken at 20260217102911

Explain:
"This restores only TS2, not the full database."

--------------------------------------------------
5. ROLLFORWARD TO END OF LOGS
--------------------------------------------------

db2 rollforward db KSLAB1 \
to end of logs \
and complete \
tablespace (TS2) \
online

Explain:
"This replays transaction logs and brings TS2 to the
latest consistent point."

--------------------------------------------------
6. VERIFY THE RESULT
--------------------------------------------------

db2 list tablespaces show detail

db2 "select * from T_TS2 order by ID"

Explain:
"The full tablespace is now available again, and all data
inside TS2 has been recovered."

--------------------------------------------------
7. CLOSING MESSAGE
--------------------------------------------------

Explain:
"This is a faster and more targeted recovery method than
restoring the entire database, which is especially important
for large DB2 environments."

--------------------------------------------------
8. COPY/PASTE BLOCK
--------------------------------------------------

db2 connect to KSLAB1
db2 "select * from T_TS2 order by ID"
db2 list history backup all for KSLAB1
db2 "set tablespace TS2 offline"
db2 restore db KSLAB1 tablespace (TS2) online from /ars/home/kslab/backup/full/tgt1,/ars/home/kslab/backup/full/tgt2 taken at 20260217102911
db2 rollforward db KSLAB1 to end of logs and complete tablespace (TS2) online
db2 list tablespaces show detail
db2 "select * from T_TS2 order by ID"
